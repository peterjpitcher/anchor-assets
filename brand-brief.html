Cosy Comforts Tasting Night

Friday, November 7th, 2025 • 7:00 – 10:30 PM • Private Dining Room

Overview

T’was the night before Christmas at The Anchor: candles aglow, winter spices drifting through the room, and friends gathered close against the chill. Cosy Comforts invites guests into that feeling—six seasonal spirits discovered blind, stories shared, and favourites revealed—paired with an autumnal plated meal that warms the table and the heart. It’s an intimate, memory‑making evening where your brand becomes part of the story guests tell all through December.

  • Capacity: 25 guests
  • Format: Blind tasting + autumnal meal + quiz + demo
Festive Crimson #DC143C
Warm Gold #D4AF37

Core Concept

A premium blind tasting of six seasonal spirits ideal for the festive season. Focus on spirits (not liqueurs), paired with an autumnal plated meal to create a warm, communal atmosphere that flatters each serve.

Product Lineup

Suggested six tastings — request 2×70cl per brand partner (25ml serves + prizes). POS giveaways are optional; if provided, please supply 25 units (see Partner Requests).

  • Spiced/Dark Rum: Rich molasses and spice notes
  • Single Malt Whisky: Peated or sherried fireplace sipper
  • Premium Cognac/Brandy: Smooth, warming after‑dinner profile
  • Barrel‑Aged Gin: Oak‑influenced complexity
  • Premium Vodka: Characterful craft base (rye/wheat)
  • Bourbon/American Whisky: Sweet vanilla, Christmas‑friendly

Mixers & Garnish Premium ginger beer/ale, apple juice, tonic variants, bitters; seasonal garnishes (apple, cinnamon, rosemary).
